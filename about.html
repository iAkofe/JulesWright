Jules
Wright

Julius Wright is the father of two teenage boys and a former professor of education. He lives with his wife, two sons, three dogs, and a cat in rural Massachusetts in a bucolic town.

Jules has a doctorate in educational philosophy from Virginia Tech and studied history and classics at William and Mary. Dr. Wright is of East Saxon lineage and has contemplated what life may have been like at the end of the classic period and the beginning of the Sub-Roman Period.
